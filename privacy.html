Privacy Policy

Spellora respects your privacy. This app is designed to be safe for children and transparent for parents.

Information We Collect

Spellora does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Microphone Access

The app uses the device microphone only to listen to spelling attempts during practice sessions.

Audio is processed on the device only and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted off the device.

Children’s Privacy

Spellora is suitable for children and does not require accounts, names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers.

Third-Party Services

Spellora does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third parties.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, updates will be posted on this page.
