Designed for children's safety

Spellora does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app is built from the ground up to be safe for children of all ages.

📋 Information We Collect

Spellora does not collect, store, or share any personal data about you or your child.

All spelling practice data — profiles, progress, streaks, and word lists — is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted anywhere.

🎙️ Microphone & Voice Recognition

Spellora uses your device's microphone to listen to spelling attempts during practice sessions.

  • Audio is processed on your device only using Apple's Speech framework
  • Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted off the device
  • Voice recognition requires microphone permission, which you can revoke at any time in iOS Settings
  • No voice data is sent to Spellora or any third party

📊 Analytics

Spellora uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics tool, to understand how the app is used and improve the experience.

  • TelemetryDeck collects anonymised usage signals only (e.g. "a session was started")
  • No personally identifiable information is ever collected
  • Data is not linked to any user identity
  • Data is never used for advertising or tracking
  • TelemetryDeck is fully COPPA compliant

👶 Children's Privacy

Spellora is designed for children and takes children's privacy very seriously.

  • No accounts, names, email addresses, or personal identifiers are required
  • Child profiles are stored locally on device only
  • No child data is ever transmitted or shared
  • Spellora complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

🤝 Third-Party Services

Spellora uses only the following third-party service:

  • TelemetryDeck — privacy-first analytics (anonymised, no personal data)
  • Apple Speech Framework — on-device voice recognition only

Spellora does not use advertising networks, social media SDKs, or any data brokers.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, updates will be posted on this page with an updated date. We will never make changes that reduce your privacy protections without clear notice.
