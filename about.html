About Spellora

A calm, voice-powered spelling coach built with care for children and families.

← Back to home

🌟 What is Spellora?

Spellora is a voice-powered spelling bee coach for children aged 6 to 13. Children hear a word spoken aloud and say each letter one at a time — exactly like a real spelling bee.

Apple's Speech Recognition checks each letter in real time, giving instant feedback through Spello, the friendly mascot who reacts to every attempt with encouragement and celebration.

"Many spelling apps are noisy, competitive, or overwhelming. Spellora is intentionally different — calm, focused, and pressure-free."

💡 Why we built it

Spelling practice doesn't have to mean staring at a screen typing letters. Real spelling bees are spoken — children should practise the same way they'll be tested.

Spellora brings the spelling bee experience home, making it easy for children to practise independently with a patient, encouraging coach that never gets tired or frustrated.

📚 What's inside

  • 467 carefully chosen British English words across four age groups (6–7, 8–9, 10–11, 12–13)
  • Voice-powered letter-by-letter spelling with real-time feedback
  • Spello mascot with idle, listening, correct, incorrect and cheering moods
  • Multi-child profiles — each child has their own progress and streak
  • Custom word lists — add school homework words for personalised practice
  • Word of the Day with a fun fact to spark curiosity
  • My Progress screen with streaks, accuracy and words completed
  • Type mode for children who prefer keyboard input
  • Completely free — no ads, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions

🔒 Our values

Spellora is built around a simple belief: children deserve apps that respect their privacy and put learning first.

  • All voice recognition happens on-device — no audio ever leaves your phone
  • No accounts, no sign-ups, no personal data collected
  • No advertising of any kind
  • Fully COPPA compliant and designed for children's safety
  • Free forever — every child deserves access regardless of budget
