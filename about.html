About Spellora

Spellora is a calm, focused spelling practice app designed for children.

It helps learners improve spelling confidence through listening, repetition, and gentle feedback — without distractions.

Why Spellora?

Many spelling apps are noisy, competitive, or overwhelming. Spellora is intentionally different.

It is designed to feel clear, supportive, and pressure-free.

Designed for Families

Spellora does not require accounts, does not show ads, and does not track users.

Practice happens entirely on the device.

Our Philosophy

Learning works best when children feel safe, focused, and encouraged.

Spellora is built around that belief.
